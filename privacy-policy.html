This Chrome Extension is designed to help users upload competition videos from their Google Drive to YouTube and optionally organize them into playlists. This tool is not affiliated with or endorsed by RoadToNationals.com.

What Information Do We Access?

This extension uses your Google account (via OAuth 2.0) to temporarily access the following information with your permission:

  • File names and IDs of videos in your selected Google Drive folders
  • Video metadata (e.g., title, ID, and upload status) associated with your YouTube account
  • Optionally, playlists to which you add videos

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as your name, email address, contacts, or account details.

Where Is Your Data Stored?

  • Access tokens are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local for the duration of your session.
  • No user data is transmitted to or stored on any external servers.
  • We do not track, log, analyze, or monetize your usage behavior in any way.

Is Your Data Shared?

No. Your Google Drive and YouTube data is never shared with third parties. The extension only uses your account data to complete the video upload tasks that you initiate. Any videos uploaded to YouTube remain private or unlisted unless you choose otherwise.

Authentication & Permissions

We use official Google APIs for secure authentication and access:

  • identity: To authenticate your Google account
  • storage: To store session data temporarily
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly: To list and access your videos in Drive
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.force-ssl: To upload videos and manage playlists on your YouTube account
