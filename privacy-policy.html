This Chrome Extension allows users to upload gymnastics competition videos from their Google Drive to YouTube, optionally organizing them into playlists. This tool is not affiliated with RoadToNationals.com.

What data do we collect?

None. We do not collect or store any personal or sensitive user information.

OAuth Access

This extension uses Google OAuth to request permission to access:

  • Read-only access to the user's Google Drive (to find video files)
  • Upload permissions to the user's YouTube account (to upload videos and manage playlists)

All access is done with the user's explicit consent. Tokens are stored only temporarily in browser storage for the duration of the session.

Where is data stored?

No personal data is stored. Access tokens are held only in chrome.storage.local during active use and never sent to any third-party server.

What is shared?

Nothing is shared with external services. Video content remains on the user's Google Drive and YouTube accounts. No analytics or user behavior tracking is implemented.

Permissions Used

  • identity: for OAuth login
  • storage: to store temporary OAuth access tokens
  • host_permissions: to communicate with Google APIs
