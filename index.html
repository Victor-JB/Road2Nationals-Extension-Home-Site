GymACT Road2Nationals Content Uploader

This Chrome Extension helps coaches, athletes, and managers upload gymnastics competition videos directly from Google Drive to YouTube. It can also organize videos into playlists, simplifying workflows for RoadToNationals.com (not affiliated).

This site hosts the privacy documentation for the Chrome Extension used to automate video uploads to YouTube and optionally assist with RoadToNationals content management.
